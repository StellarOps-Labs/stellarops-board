# Code of Conduct

StellarOps Board follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, clear, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and repeated disruptive behavior are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
